We're excited to launch the new Arrowhead Research website. This platform will serve as a hub for our community — sharing updates on programs, insights from the field, and stories from the servicemembers driving innovation at the tactical edge.

## Why We're Here

Arrowhead Research exists because the people closest to the problem should be the ones solving it. Military servicemembers encounter technical challenges every day, yet the pathway from identifying a problem to building a solution has traditionally been long, bureaucratic, and disconnected from operational reality.

We're changing that.

## What's Coming

Over the coming months, we'll be sharing:

- **Program updates** from Moonshot Rodeo, Black Relay, and our technical competition teams
- **Technical write-ups** from members who are building real systems
- **Event recaps** and lessons learned from the field
- **Opportunities** to get involved, whether you're active duty, guard, reserve, or a veteran

## Get Involved

If you're a servicemember with technical curiosity and a bias for action, we want to hear from you. Head to our [Contact page](/contact) to reach out, or follow along here as we share what we're building.

Build. Break. Hack. Repeat.
